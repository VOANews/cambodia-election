Hun Sen’s Dirty Dozen?

A Human Rights Watch report says 12 security officers formed the backbone of Hun Sen’s “abusive and authoritarian political regime.” According to the 213-page HRW report, titled “Cambodia’s Dirty Dozen: A Long History of Rights Abuses by Hun Sen’s Generals”, the 12 security officers “demonstrated a willingness to commit rights abuses on behalf of Hun Sen.”
